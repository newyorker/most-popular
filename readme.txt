Generate a module called Most Popular to show the most visited stories on a site

== Installation ==
1. Upload \"most-popular\" directory to the \"/wp-content/plugins/\" directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the \"Plugins\" menu in WordPress.
1. Place \"most_popular_insert();\" in your template.
1. Verify
